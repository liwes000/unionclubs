Science NHS

Contact

Science National Honor Society is a club for Union High School students interested in exploring science through service. We strive to promote science by supporting other science clubs, recognizing our exceptional science students, and encouraging the learning of science here at Union! We have partnerships with other Union clubs - such as STEM Academy and the Titan Tribune - in our pursuit of these goals. As a club, we encourage our members to participate in at least one of our yearly projects, and continue to be involved in making a difference at Union! In SNHS, we value communication, so if you are interested in our club, please contact one of our presidents/advisors!

More Information

  • There is a application needed. The application deadline has passed, however if you are interested in being a part of the club, please contact one of our presidents or advisors!
  • As this is a national honor society, we must conform to national requirements. We require four hours of service per year, and we are generous in offering them. Additionally, we try to conform the schedule of our meetings to the availability of our members, and we do ask that all members attend our meetings. Members will in no way be removed from the club for missing a meeting, however we do ask for communication to our presidents and/or advisors.
