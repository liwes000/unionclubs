More Information

The membership fee is $15 to join. As part of the club, there are two major events throughout the year: Fall Leadership Conference (FLC) and State Leadership Conference (SLC). FLC is a day trip to Seattle at the beginning of the year where members go to learn about HOSA and state events, highly recommended for new members. However, the year-long goal for the club is to prepare for SLC, which is a 3-day conference in spring where students meet and compete in a variety of medical-related events for skills like presentation, public speaking, and testing (among others). A third possible event is International Leadership Conference in June, which students are able to attend if they qualify in State. Commitment is really dependent on the individual; you can join just to see what we're about, come to our volunteer events, or take things really seriously and do intense prep for State. If you are interested in anything medical related, or hope to find out more about what our club does, please come and check out our club! Hope to see you :)
