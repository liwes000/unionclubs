More Information

    Chess Club is a student run, informal chess club at Union High School that normally happens at lunch. The goal of the club is to teach people how to play chess and get new people into the game. It is also a time for people to play with each other in a fun atmosphere. Some students are very competitive and will play each other in a much more formal way. Through this others are exposed to how competitive chess is played while connecting with others on campus who enjoy the game.
